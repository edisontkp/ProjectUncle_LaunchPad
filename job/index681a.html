Job Description

As an Assistant Office Manager at Lynk, you will be an integral part of the organization and work directly with the management team and other team members to drive internal office operations and finance operations management.

This position requires an extraordinarily driven individual who wants to play a leading role in Lynk’s execution of its daily operational activities and finance operations. It is an essential part of our business that touches upon every other function and will be a key driver in our long-term success. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to work alongside the wider Lynk team on cross-functional projects of strategic importance.

From Day to Day
Provide a full spectrum of professional office management for the HK office e.g. office supplies replenishment
Provide support for daily accounting operations and all payment-related duties including all banking related matters – invoicing, payment (e-payment/cash/cheque), expense reimbursement, etc.
Provide support for setting up office procedures and required applications
Responsible for vendor/office lease negotiation and management for HK office and overseas offices in Singapore and Mumbai
Responsible for maintaining day to day bookkeeping, administrative, secretarial and personnel services for the company
Assist to arrange team-building events e.g. birthday, team meetings and yearly corporate event
Identify improvement opportunities for financial & operational procedures, including implementing innovative payment solutions that result in greater efficiency & cost savings
Handle ad hoc administrative and financial duties
What We Are Looking For
Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Business, Economics, or related discipline
1+ years of fulltime experience in a hands-on accounting/finance role, with solid experience in office management support is a plus
Exceptional work ethic and written & verbal communication skills
Strong critical thinking & problem-solving skills, and highly adept at time management and multi-tasking
Independent & gritty self-starter that also excels in small teams and thrives in a fast-paced & evolving environment

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced leader to be a key part of a management team that is driving tremendous growth in an exciting post-Series A start-up. Reporting to the CEO, you will be leading all internal operations and drive results by unleashing creativity and accountability in a performance-oriented culture amongst a remarkably diverse and talented team. You will take responsibility for functions including but not limited to client management, account growth, user acquisition, recruitment, compliance, systems and operational efficiency.

We seek individuals with the entrepreneurial mindset, ambition, discipline and decisiveness to get things done. We value a commitment to excellence, immense intellectual curiosity, a deep understanding of how businesses operate and an all-around emphasis on quality of the client/expert experience. You are attracted to constantly finding solutions to new problems that you have never encountered before. Problem-solving in an uncertain environment is one of your core strengths.

A successful candidate will have razor-sharp focus on execution, implementation and operational efficiency, relentless growth and building a world-class team to deliver amazing experiences for leading institutional clients ranging from management consultancies, investment firms and corporates, as well as experts from diverse industries globally.

From Day to Day
Business Strategy, Planning and Implementation:
• Play a leading role in developing and implementing strategic plans that support Lynk’s vision and goals, communicate strategic plans to the broader team to ensure buy-in, and reshape strategy in response to changing internal and external factors to reach Lynk’s financial goals.
• Set comprehensive goals and operating metrics for Lynk’s performance and growth, lead the performance management process by evaluating and analyzing operating results against goals (metrics, revenues, costs, profits, etc), and identify new value creation opportunities for the business and recommend action plans.
• Analyze current and future trends and needs, and accurately assesses functional responsibilities, competitive strengths and vulnerabilities.
Client Solutions and Account Growth:
• Develop relationships with key client contacts in core existing client accounts in-line with strategic growth plans.
• Work with the Sales team to devise and implement sales plans and targets and direct collaboration between the Sales and Client Solutions teams to ensure acquisition of new clients and account expansion/renewals.
• Manages overall day-to-day operations (Client Solutions, Sales, Marketing, Compliance) to meet growth targets and budget plans, and ensure the superior quality of end-to-end client project delivery by the Client Solutions team.
Operational Efficiency:
• Assess effectiveness and efficiency of operational processes and systems across the organization, and find ways to constantly improve operational efficiency to meet the company’s rapid growth objectives.
• Lead budgeting and resource allocation efforts alongside the senior management team.
Team Building:
• Build and manage a streamlined organizational structure and operating practices that are adaptable to evolving business needs; leads formulation and enforcement of company policies.
• Work with the HR team to recruit the best talent and retain top performers in building a world-class team, develop and implement a talent roadmap to meet Lynk’s strategic needs.
• Manage employee performance by establishing clear goals and expectations, track progress against objectives, provide timely feedback, and address performance issues promptly.
• Provide mentoring and motivate the team to meet Lynk’s ambitious goals, manage and constantly improve training programs for new and existing team members so we can better our platform, product/service offerings and client service.
Ensure all initiatives and activities align with the company’s core values and culture, and promote and safeguard Lynk’s values and philosophy on ethics, compliance and integrity.
Oversee risk management and legal activities with the Compliance team including client contracts, partnership agreements, leases, etc.
What We Are Looking For
Proven experience as Director/Head of Operations, Chief Operating Officer or similar role.
Strong passion for business and enthusiastic about shaping LYNK’s growth.
Successful track record in significantly scaling a business’ operations.
Deep understanding of business functions including operations, client management, sales, HR, finance, marketing, compliance, tech, etc.
Demonstrated experience in operational execution, strategic and financial planning and analysis, and business development.
Superior leadership skills in team/culture building, organizational development, people management, budget and resource planning.
Must have 5+ years of strong operational experience and ideally worked in a senior management role for 3+ years.
Experience in managing a start-up, participating in fundraising and working in a fast-growing international environment is preferred.

Job Description

As a Finance Associate at Lynk, you will be an integral part of the finance team based in Hong Kong and work directly with the CFO to drive financial analysis and problem-solving. Lynk is a fast-growing VC-backed startup with a highly talented & motivated multinational team spread across HK, Singapore & Mumbai.

This position requires an extraordinarily driven individual who wants to play a leading role in Lynk’s execution of its financial strategy. Finance is an essential part of our business that touches upon every other function and will be a key driver in our long-term success. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to work alongside the wider Lynk team on cross-functional projects of strategic importance.

From Day to Day
Work closely with the CFO and other senior management to support strategic execution and analysis
Drive the evaluation of key performance indicators & metrics to assess financial and operational performance
Support the development & analysis of quantitative models that facilitate data-driven decision-making
Liaise with external accountants & auditors in preparing & reconciling company financials and audit reports
Collaborate with partner organizations (e.g. Wework, Cyberport, various incubators/accelerators) to support partnership initiatives
Manage client invoicing & settlement, expert/employee/vendor payment processes, and cash/banking/e-payment accounts
Identify improvement opportunities for financial & operational procedures, including implementing innovative payment solutions that result in greater efficiency & cost savings
Draft business & financial updates for existing investors and contribute to all stages of fundraising activities, when applicable
Handle ad hoc administrative and financial duties
What We Are Looking For
Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance, Business, Economics, or related discipline
2+ years of fulltime experience in a hands-on accounting/finance role, with proven track record of driving financial reporting & analysis in support of management decision-making
Exceptional work ethic and written & verbal communication skills
Strong critical thinking & problem-solving skills, and highly adept at time management and multi-tasking
Independent & gritty self-starter that also excels in small teams and thrives in a fast-paced & evolving environment
