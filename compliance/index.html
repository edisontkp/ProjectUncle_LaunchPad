Compliance
We’ll make sure all of your compliance requirements are met.
Compliance Framework

We have implemented robust framework and technology based on industry best practices to supplement and enhance your compliance protocols, helping you reduce your exposure to potential conflicts.

Comprehensive Vetting and Verification of Experts

All experts are carefully vetted and verified through desktop research, database searches, and reference checks. All personal data and interactions are recorded, allowing for active monitoring and audits.

Custom usage reports can be generated upon request.

Customizable Compliance System
No consultation can be arranged unless all compliance requirements are met within the system. We also provide customizable compliance processes, as necessary.
Rigorous Compliance Training
All Lynk staff undergoes month-long orientation training, including Code of Ethics & Conduct, compliance training, and systems. We utilize a proprietary platform to manage expert recruitment and selection process with custom compliance controls.
Detailed Post-Consultation Feedback
Lynk obtains feedback from both clients and experts post consultation on whether compliance standards were upheld by both parties. Feedback is then factored into future expert selection processes.
