Trends and Opportunities in Mobile Ecosystem

20/09/2013

Speaker: Rajive Chandra

Position: Director of RAC Consulting Pte Ltd

Topics:

Overview of industry trends and differences in usage behavior across ASEAN
Growth opportunities among the players in the region - device manufacturers, app developers, network operators
Where is the industry heading? Discussion on consolidation opportunities

Background:

Knowledge Partner has over 30 years of experience in building consumer technology and FMCG businesses in both B2B and B2C segments. He has been the Director of RAC Consulting Pte Ltd since January 2013 and Director of Consumer and Technology Practice at ANV Consulting Pte Ltd since June 2011.

Previously he served as Head of Sales and Marketing Asia Pacific at TomTom International B.V. (July 2009 - June 2011) which is the world’s leading provider of location and navigation solutions for smartphones and tablets. He had also worked in Asia Pacific region as Director of Strategy and Business Development at Motorola (May 1995 - April 2004) and as General Manager of Consumer Sales and Marketing at Dell (December 2007 - December 2010).
